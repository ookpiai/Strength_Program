General Notes & Reminders:

  • Warm-Up: Use dynamic mobility for ankles, hips, T-spine, shoulders. Light sets to prepare.
  • Max Strength Lifts: Keep reps in 1–5 range, rest 2–3 minutes. Increase loads slowly.
  • Explosive/Dynamic: ~50–70% 1RM with maximum speed. Rest 60–90 seconds to maintain power.
  • Rotational & SSC: Emphasize hip-then-torso coil/uncoil. Relax until final impact.
  • Isometrics: Use for grappling strength (planks, bridging, split-squat holds).
  • Recovery & Skill: Account for sparring/rolling volume. Adjust days if fatigued.
